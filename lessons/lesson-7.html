Lesson #7
Cleaning is overwhelming and the reward of cleanliness is not strong enough to start
2025.11.20

Episode 7 of Ginpachi-sensei is about cleaning the school and the title suggests that you can get distracted by interesting finds when you clean, so in the end you don't get much cleaning done. Starting is hard enough, what more the actual cleaning. In the episode, Ginpachi-sensei shows a clipping from a dodgy newspaper which rewards schools for finding curious treasures while cleaning. I need that. We all need that.
