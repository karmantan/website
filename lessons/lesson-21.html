Lesson #21
If you rush, things get confusing, but if you take your time, many people are inconvenienced
2026.04.03

Caption

The second part of Fire Force Season 3 is quite confusing. There was a nice buildup up to the end of Season 3 part 1, but it really feels like part 2 is rushed. There are some plot relevant details that seem somewhat random, and the audience is expected to just accept it. Like I am struggling to understand how imagination, doppelgängers, Adolla, the Great Cataclysm all fit together. Maybe I'm slow...

Meanwhile, you have Steel Ball Run, i.e. part 7 or "Season 6" of JoJo, which came over 3 years after Stone Ocean. I haven't watched anything this exciting since Golden Kamuy and and there's just no... second episode after two weeks? OK, this is barely an inconvenience - I'll get to that - but, come on...

In terms of inconvenience, there's a "finished" bridge in Brandenburg-Wust which has an access ramp on ONE side... LOL... Apparently, people have to use a railway crossing which not only causes traffic, but the wait time can be 20 minutes on average. So, of course, an overpass makes sense. The said overpass was completed in 2023 (relatively quickly, I might add, since construction began in 2022). But the reason it lacked a ramp on one side was because you need to apply for a construction permit for the land where the ramp goes on. Applying for a construction permit for the bridge "alone" isn't sufficient. LOL. Nevermind that, eventually in 2024, the construction for the ramp was then tendered but stalled because some morons from noise-protection companies opposed this construction (what's a noise protection company???). Now, apparently another tendering process has been completed and some noise protection company is involved in constructing the noise barrier. (So what was the problem in the first place?) The ramp is set to be completed in 2027. I first heard about this story on "Realer Irrsinn".
