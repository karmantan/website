Lesson #12
Don't trust self-proclaimed perfectionists
2025.12.22

Caption

Because they're really not above cutting corners. In fact, they're even more likely to cut corners because they will be dramatic about how stressed out they are about making everything perfect. So, when you get the final product and it's garbage, you can't say anything for fear of piling on to their stress.
