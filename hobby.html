Hobby

Since the release of 3年Z組銀八先生 anime, I started sharing random "lessons" on my Instagram stories based on weekly reflections and observations. A conversation with a friend inspired me to expand on these lessons as a writing exercise. The idea is to eventually create a webcomic. I am not good at drawing or storytelling so I want to start by writing a paragraph on what led to the lesson of the week.
